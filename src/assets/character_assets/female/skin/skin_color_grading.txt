Skin color grading

| Level | Name          | Represents                          |
| ----- | ------------- | ----------------------------------- |
| 1     | **Ivory**     | Very light / pale tone              |
| 2     | **Sandstone** | Light tan / fair beige              |
| 3     | **Bronze**    | Medium brown / golden brown         |
| 4     | **Umber**     | Deep brown / chocolatey tone        |
| 5     | **Onyx**      | Very dark brown / nearly black skin |
